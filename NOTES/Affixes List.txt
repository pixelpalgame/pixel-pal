DETAILED CORE MECHANICS GLOSSARY
================================
These are foundational game systems. Affixes interact with them. Implement as global modifiers.

- **Damage Types**:
  - Raw (physical): Direct melee/ranged hits. Mitigated by Hardiness.
  - Flame (fire): Burning DoT + direct. Ignites flammables.
  - Frost (cold): Slows enemies, chills (reduces action speed).
  - Storm (lightning): Chains to nearby foes, shocks (stuns briefly).
  - Toxin (poison): Pure DoT, stacks for Wither synergy.
  - Umbra (shadow): Ignores partial defenses, fears (run chance).
  - Void (chaos): Bypasses most resists, corrupts (heals caster on kill).
  - Arcane (pure magic): Spell-only, amplified by Mind.

- **Sunder**: Debuff on enemies. Increases damage taken from ALL sources by 10-20% per stack (up to 3 stacks). Duration: 4s per stack (refreshes). Applied via "Sunder Infliction Chance" or skills. Visual: Glowing cracks on enemy model. Interaction: Multiplies with other debuffs (additive). Bosses resist stacking.

- **Crush**: Burst damage mechanic. On qualifying hits (e.g., full charge or vs armored), adds flat bonus damage (ignores 50% enemy Hardiness). Procced by "Crush Potency" affixes. Visual: Hammer-like impact shatter. Interaction: Scales with Raw/Storm damage.

- **Wither**: DoT effect. Deals X damage/sec for 6s, stacks up to 10 (each adds 50% more). Synergizes with Toxin. Visual: Purple haze/rotting flesh. "Wither Potency" boosts total output.

- **Rebuke**: Passive reflection. Returns 20-50% of incoming damage to attacker (scales with affix value). Triggers on any hit. Visual: Spike deflection particles. Caps at 100% total reflect. Interaction: Doesn't trigger on DoTs.

- **Rampart**: Absorb shield. Builds X absorption per hit taken/blocked (up to 50% max Vitality). Lasts 10s, decays 10%/sec if not hit. Visual: Glowing barrier. "Rampart Accrual" increases build rate.

- **Lethal (Critical Strikes)**: Hits do 150% base multiplier + Lethal Multiplier. "Lethal Chance" adds to global crit rate (cap 100%). Procs Fateful Strikes. Visual: Red flash, slow-mo.

- **Strike Tempo**: Global attack speed multiplier for weapon strikes (melee/ranged). +10% = 10% faster attacks.

- **Invocation Tempo**: Cast speed for spells/abilities. Affects channel time, cooldowns indirectly.

- **Stride Speed**: Out-of-combat/combat movement multiplier.

- **Vitality**: Health pool. Depletes to 0 = death. Regen via affixes/skills.

- **Essence**: Resource pool for abilities. Regens via Flux.

- **Flux**: Essence per second regen rate.

- **Hardiness**: Flat physical mitigation (reduces Raw by % based on rating vs enemy attack).

- **Dodge**: % chance to fully evade attacks (physical/spells).

- **Veil**: Separate shield pool that absorbs all damage before Vitality. Regens out of combat or via skills.

- **Traits**:
  - Brawn: +Melee Raw dmg, +Hardiness.
  - Agility: +Ranged Raw dmg, +Dodge/Stride.
  - Mind: +Arcane/Elemental dmg, +Veil/Essence.
  - Resolve: +All defenses, +Vitality/Essence regen.

- **Shatter**: Enemy reaches 0 Vitality = shatter. Triggers on-kill effects.

- **Strike**: Any successful hit on enemy.

- **Armor Shred**: Reduces enemy Hardiness by X flat for 5s (stacks).

- **Precision Rating**: +X% hit chance vs high-Dodge enemies.

- **Ranks**: +1 Rank = 20% more damage/effect on that ability group. Caps at +5/item.

- **Fateful Strike**: RNG proc (5-20% base). Triggers extras: bonus loot, temp buffs, mini-explosions.

- **Immunities (Resists)**: % damage reduction per type (cap 90%). "All Elements" = Flame/Frost/Storm/Toxin/Umbra.

TIERS & ROLLING

- Mid (21-50): +20-50, conditionals (e.g., "on Shatter").
- High (51+): +50-150, uniques (e.g., "Sunder on Crit").
