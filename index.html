⚔ STATS & AFFIX REFERENCE
✕
⚔ OFFENSIVE
Raw DmgAdds flat damage to every attack, stacking directly on top of your weapon's base damage. No conditions — always active.
Strike TempoReduces attack animation length, letting you swing more frequently. Higher values noticeably increase your damage output over time.
Lethal Chance %Each point gives a flat % chance to trigger a Lethal Hit. A Lethal Hit multiplies your damage by the Lethal Mult affix (or 1.5× by default).
Lethal MultDetermines how hard a Lethal Hit hits. For example, 15 = 1.15× damage on a Lethal. Pair with Lethal Chance for burst damage.
Sunder Chance %Chance on hit to apply Sunder to the target, temporarily reducing their defense. Sundered enemies take more damage from all sources.
Crush PotencyAmplifies Crush effects, dealing bonus damage to heavily armored targets. More effective the more armor the enemy has.
Wither PotencyAmplifies Wither effects, reducing enemy stats over time on hit. Stacks with multiple hits before expiring.
Armor ShredPermanently strips a portion of the enemy's armor rating per hit during the fight. Makes each subsequent hit deal more damage.
Fateful Strike %Each point adds a 3% chance to deal a 1.5× critical hit. Fully independent from Lethal — both can trigger on the same attack.
🛡 DEFENSIVE
HardinessFlat damage reduction on every hit you receive. Each point reduces incoming damage by ~0.25. Effective against fast, low-hit enemies.
VitalityIncreases your maximum HP. Simple and reliable — more total health means more room for mistakes.
Vit RegenSlowly restores HP over time while you are out of active combat. The higher the value, the faster you recover between fights.
VeilCreates an absorption barrier that takes hits before your HP does. Recharges when you haven't taken damage for a short time.
Dodge %Flat chance to completely avoid an incoming attack. A dodge negates all damage from that hit. Effective against single large hits.
RebukeReflects a small amount of damage back at any enemy that strikes you. Passive — no action required. Scales with the value.
RampartReduces the chip damage that bleeds through your block. Without Rampart, blocking still deals chip damage. High values make blocking very efficient.
All Resist %Reduces ALL incoming damage by a percentage before other reductions. Stacks multiplicatively, capped at 80%. Best-in-slot defensive affix for bosses.
✦ UTILITY
Stride SpeedIncreases your movement speed. Useful for dodging attacks manually, exploring faster, and repositioning in combat.
EssenceIncreases your maximum Essence pool. Essence is consumed by active abilities — a larger pool means more uses before needing to regenerate.
FluxIncreases your Essence regeneration rate. Pairs with Essence Max — having a bigger pool matters less if it refills slowly.
◈ TRAITS
BrawnScales physical damage output and increases carry weight. Good for heavy weapons. Each point provides a compound bonus across multiple damage formulas.
AgilityImproves attack speed scaling and contributes to your base Dodge chance. Favours light weapon builds and hit-and-run playstyles.
MindBoosts skill XP gain rates and expands your Essence pool passively. Important for progression builds focused on levelling skills fast.
ResolveImproves HP regeneration rate and reduces the duration of debuffs applied to you. Best in long, sustained fights.
💢 ON-HIT
Vit on HitRestores HP equal to this value on each successful hit you land. Aggressive fighters benefit more — the more hits you deal, the more you heal.
⛏ GATHERING
Mine SpeedReduces the cooldown between mining swings. Directly increases how many ore strikes you can land per minute in the Mines.
YieldIncreases the quantity of ore received from each successful mine strike. Stacks with base yield — essential for efficient resource farming.
ReachExtends the effective range of your mining tool, allowing you to hit ore veins from further away and strike multiple nodes per swing.

PREVIEW
☠ DOWNTOWN WARDEN
Boss Fight — Learn The Mechanics
The Warden has three learnable attack patterns. Read them before you step in — they can all be dodged once you know what to look for.
🔴
WINDUP SWING
The Warden flashes red before striking. Block Q just before the hit lands — or back away out of range.
⚡
CHARGE RUSH
He winds up, then sprints across the entire arena. Jump Space over him to avoid the hit — blocking alone won't stop the force.
💥
GROUND SLAM
He leaps into the air then crashes down, sending shockwaves along the floor. Jump Space over the wave — standing on the ground will hurt you.
🔥
RAGE MODE — 50% HP
Below half health the Warden enters a fury — faster, harder hits, double shockwaves on slam. A red border appears. Stay mobile.
